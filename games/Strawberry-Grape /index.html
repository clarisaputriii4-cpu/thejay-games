🎮 HOW TO PLAY
1. Use PLANT tool to sow random seeds (-10 Gold).
2. Check the soil to see the seeds! Use WATER to sprout them.
3. Grab HARVEST once Strawberries 🍓 or Grapes 🍇 are ripe (+30 Gold).
🚨 GAME OVER CONDITIONS
• Bankruptcy: Gold drops below 10 AND you have no growing/ripe crops left.
• Soft Lock: Your Gold is too low to clean rotted crops (-5G).
